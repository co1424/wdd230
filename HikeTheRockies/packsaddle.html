Packsaddle Lake Trail

📍 How to get there?

Discover this 0.8-mile out-and-back trail near Driggs, Idaho. Generally considered an easy route, it takes an average of 29 min to complete. This is a popular trail for hiking, trail running, and walking, but you can still enjoy some solitude during quieter times of day. The best times to visit this trail are May through September. Dogs are welcome, but must be on a leash.
