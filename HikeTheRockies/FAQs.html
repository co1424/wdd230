FAQs:

Got questions? Here we go!

Can these hikes be done during winter?
Yes! They can, we recommend you get a pair of spikes in some cases, but all of them are accessible.

Can these hikes be done during the night?
Although most of the hikes are opened at night, we do NOT recommend doing it. It can be dangerous due to animals and limited visibility, especially if you bring kids with you.

Are there any kid friendly trails?
Yes! You will find that in each hike description.

What kind of food do you recommend bringing?
Bring plenty of water. Energy/granola bars and fruit are a good idea as well.
