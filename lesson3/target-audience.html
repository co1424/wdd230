Target Audience:

Persona One:

Local business owner who wants to interact or partner with other local businesses. Business person who wants to become available to the community. This individual might also be looking for new business ideas as well and knows that local businesses can be found all in one place.

Persona Two:

New family moved into the area with the hope to open a new business. They may want to come here for information about businesses in the area, along with questions about what could be more profitable right now.

Potential Scenarios:

    What do I need to do to have my business be part of this organization?

    Individual or business looking for beusiness advicing.

    People from out of town looking for recommendations on what kind of business to open or invest on in the area according to the community's needs.

    General questions about what I can or cannot do in my business in a specific area.

    Complaints.

    Business proposals to the city.
